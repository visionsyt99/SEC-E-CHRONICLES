The Origin

How Section E came to be.

25
Batch
// 01.01

It started with a door. One classroom in St. Xavier's College, Maitighar — and fifty-six strangers walking in from different schools, different towns, different dreams. All landing in the same Science (Biology) section on the same morning.

// 01.02

Nobody planned to become a family. But that's exactly what happened. The early morning practicals. The shared panic before Chemistry tests. The inside jokes that only make sense if you were there — they quietly stitched fifty-six individuals into one unit.

// 01.03

Section E isn't just a roll call from 501 to 556. It's a living, breathing community that challenges each other to grow and refuses to let anyone walk this journey alone.

// 01.04

This website — The E Chronicles — is our shared archive. A place to document every moment of these two extraordinary years, so that no story gets left behind.
