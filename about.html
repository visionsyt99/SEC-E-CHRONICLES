// Who we are

The Section E Story

Section E is one of the science sections of St. Xavier's College, Maitighar, Kathmandu — one of Nepal's most prestigious higher secondary institutions. Our batch, 2025–2027, is a diverse group of 56 students brought together by a shared passion for Biology and the sciences.

We come from different backgrounds, different schools, and different corners of Nepal — but from the moment we walked into the same classroom, we became one unit. Section E isn't just a roll call. It's a community of people who challenge each other, support each other, and grow together.

This website — The E Chronicles — is our shared space. A place to document our journey, celebrate our wins, share our resources, and remember every moment of these two extraordinary years.
