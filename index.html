// Our Chapter

A section is not just a room — it's the people who fill it.

From the first nervous handshake in Grade 11 to the inside jokes that now write themselves, Section E has become more than a roster of names. It's a collective.

Biology lectures bleed into chai breaks, Physics problems get solved on canteen napkins, and somewhere between Chemistry titrations and Math derivatives, we stopped being classmates and started being family.

This is our chronicle. Fifty-six voices, two years, infinite stories.
